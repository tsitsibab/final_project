about us

How did Guitar Shop create?

Guitar Shop was created in early 2004. We wanted to create a new type of shop that could cater for players of all levels, but with particular help for beginners and intermediate players, with clear product descriptions, photographs, easy navigation and no confusing lingo! We always vowed that customer service would be our top priority, so we can concentrate all of our efforts on you. We have come a long way since our early beginnings. We have dedicated mail order facilities with large stocks of all our key products as well as a busy shop.
